Purpose and functions

Welcome to Bibliometric Visualisasion Website! The website is to be designed to visualize mainly the co-authorship pattern and the changing trend, where authors are based, which are the most productive countries, how does the main theme of the published papers changed over time, who are the most productive authors within each conference and how do these relate to other factors like citations rates.

Find out more about bibliometric!

Functions of website:

Discover Trends

Find out the keywords of each year.

Discover Co-authorship

SFind out the collaborative relationships between authors and countries.

Analysis Based on Country

Find out the productivity rank of countries.

Productivity Analysis

Find out the productivity of authors (with more than 4 publications).
